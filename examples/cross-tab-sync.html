🔄 Cross-Tab Sync Demo

Open this page in multiple tabs to see cache synchronization in action!

📌 Instructions:
  1. Open this page in 2 or more browser tabs
  2. Click "Fetch & Cache Data" in one tab
  3. Click "Read from Cache" in another tab - you'll see the data!
  4. Click "Clear Cache" in any tab - all tabs will be cleared
